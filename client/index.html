How We Met

It was a spring day in April of 2022. We had just started a new job together. We worked from home so we had not met in person but saw each other everyday on Microsoft Teams video.

As we trained and learned together, our calls started getting longer and longer as we got to know each other- we clearly enjoyed each other's company. It wasn't long before we were spending our entire 8 hour shift on video together. Working and smiling.

We first met in person in July 2022 at a Stand up comedy student showcase where Johnny got to watch Nina be silly delivering stand up comedy. After MONTHS of chatting on video all day - did we have the same chemistry in person? Ah, yes and even more! A couple months later we began dating. After months of dating and integrating families - we began building a life together.

Today, we are together nearly 24/7 and love every minute of it! Truly best friends who play games together, craft together, family activities, explore new hobbies, new places and adventures, hike, workout, just two peas in a pod! And most importantly, support and take care of each other in every way. Not a single doubt that we are forever mates and ready to make it official!
