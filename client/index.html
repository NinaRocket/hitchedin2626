How We Met

It was a spring day in April of 2022. Johnny and Nina had just started a new job together. They worked virtually so had not met in person but worked together on Microsoft Teams video.

As they trained and learned together, their calls started getting longer and longer as they got to know each other and enjoyed each other's company. It wasn't long until they were spending their entire 8 hour shift on video together. Working and smiling.

They met in person towards the end of July 2022 and that is when Nina decided she was in love and needed to make her move. A couple months later they began dating. After months of dating they began building a life together.

Today, they are together nearly 24/7 and love every minute of it! Truly best friends who play games together, craft together, family activities, explore new hobbies, new places and adventures, hike, workout, just two peas in a pod! And most importantly, support and take care of each other in every way. Not a single doubt that they are forever mates and ready to make it official!
